# Architectural Layers

Every creation exists within one or more architectural layers.

Confusing these layers creates duplication, rigidity, and unnecessary redesign.

AOS distinguishes the following layers.

---

## Layer 1 — Principles

Universal truths.

Examples:

- Wisdom precedes creation.
- Conversation precedes generation.
- Structure is independent from content.

Principles rarely change.

---

## Layer 2 — Architecture

Defines how an entire family of systems works.

Example:

Promotional Media Architecture

An architecture may produce many operating systems.

---

## Layer 3 — Operating System

Coordinates a specific domain.

Examples:

- PMOS
- Contractor Commons OS
- ManualMe
- GroundCow
- AI Boss

---

## Layer 4 — Templates

Reusable patterns.

Examples:

- Restaurant postcard
- Service postcard
- Magazine
- Door hanger
- Landing page
- Newsletter

Templates contain structure but little or no content.

---

## Layer 5 — Instances

Actual implementations.

Examples:

- St. Augustine Restaurant Founders Edition
- Jacksonville Restaurant September Edition
- Orlando Service Card October Edition

Instances contain content and state.

---

## Layer 6 — Outputs

Generated artifacts.

Examples:

- Website
- PDF
- Artwork
- Stripe Products
- Invoices
- Emails
- QR Codes
- Reports
- Printed media

Outputs are disposable.

The architecture is not.
