# AOS Default Principles

These principles are inherited by every system generated through AOS unless intentionally overridden.

---

## 1. Wisdom precedes creation.

Not every idea deserves embodiment.

AOS assists creation but never replaces discernment.

---

## 2. Conversation precedes generation.

Generation begins only after sufficient understanding has been established.

Questions are not delays.

Questions are architecture.

---

## 3. Conversation precedes iteration.

Iteration without understanding compounds mistakes.

Understanding should grow before revision.

---

## 4. Separate Wisdom, Structure, Rules, and Content.

Wisdom determines whether something should exist.

Structure defines what exists.

Rules define how it behaves.

Content fills the structure.

Changing one layer should require minimal changes to the others.

---

## 5. Prefer reusable structures.

If something is likely to be created twice, it should become a template.

---

## 6. Prefer composition over duplication.

Build from modules.

Avoid copying systems.

---

## 7. Objects before interfaces.

The underlying system should exist independently of any website, app, or API.

Interfaces change.

Systems endure.

---

## 8. Rules before automation.

Automation should execute established rules.

Automation should not invent them.

---

## 9. Human authority remains explicit.

Humans establish goals.

Humans approve significant decisions.

AI assists.

AI recommends.

AI generates.

AI does not become the owner of the system.

---

## 10. Systems evolve.

Every system should be designed to accept:

- New locations
- New templates
- New products
- New workflows
- New user roles
- New automations

without redesigning the architecture.
